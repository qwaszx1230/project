「Product ordering meeting」support agents to hold product ordering meeting in the early period of launching.

「Staff support」the company provides professional hour management staff and tightly coordinates with agents and promotes business increase together.

「Sales training」the company provides agents with sales team to carry out training on relevant product knowledge and management and increase the team fighting capacity.

「Team motivation」the company will provide reward proposal (refer to agency agreement for detail) of products distribution to sales team of agents in different markets.
